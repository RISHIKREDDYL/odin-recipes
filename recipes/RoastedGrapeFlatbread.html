Roasted Grape Flatbread

A Delicious Appetizer or Side.

Roasted Grape Flatbread is a delightful and unique recipe that combines the sweetness of roasted grapes with the savory flavors of crispy flatbread.This dish is a perfect blend of contrasting tastes and textures, making it an impressive appetizer or even a light meal.

The roasted grapes bring out their natural sugars, intensifying their flavor and adding a touch of caramelization. When paired with the thin and crispy flatbread, the result is a mouthwatering combination that will leave your taste buds craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red seedless grapes
  • 1 cup green seedless grapes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 flatbreads (store-bought or homemade)
  • 4 ounces goat cheese, crumbled
  • Fresh thyme leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400째F (200째C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the red and green grapes with olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and black pepper. Toss gently until the grapes are well coated.
  3. Transfer the grapes to a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, spreading them out in a single layer. Roast in the preheated oven for about 20 minutes, or until the grapes start to soften and release their juices.
  4. While the grapes are roasting, place the flatbreads on another baking sheet. Drizzle a little olive oil over the flatbreads and spread it evenly.
  5. Once the grapes are done, remove them from the oven and increase the oven temperature to 450째F (230째C). Place the flatbreads in the oven and bake for about 8-10 minutes or until they turn golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove the flatbreads from the oven and let them cool slightly. Spread the crumbled goat cheese evenly over the flatbreads.
  7. Spoon the roasted grapes over the goat cheese, distributing them evenly. Sprinkle fresh thyme leaves on top for an aromatic touch.
  8. Cut the flatbread into slices or wedges and serve as an appetizer or a light meal. Enjoy the unique combination of flavors and textures in every bite!
